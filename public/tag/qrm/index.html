QRM

QRM Tools
A Few Excel and R tools for Quality Risk Management. Many of the tools used in quality risk management can be implemented in spreadsheet software. Spreadsheets are widely accessible and often the preferred way to build a risk management exercise, training example or solution. This project presents several common QRM tools in a spreadsheet format.
